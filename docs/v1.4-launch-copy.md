## Greasy Fork v1.4.0 release notes

### English

SourceCapsule v1.4 is a major reliability and workflow upgrade for exporting X posts, threads, and
Articles.

What's new:

- Strict Export is enabled by default. It detects visible dead ends, retries recovery, and warns
  before an incomplete archive is downloaded.
- Quote recovery now uses X-delivered GraphQL references plus syndication data to restore missing
  permalinks and rebuild quote cards that never mounted in the DOM.
- Nested quotes, polls, rich external-link cards, thumbnails, and quoted media survive recovery.
- Long Articles progressively load from the top, preserving lazy images even when export starts
  mid-Article.
- Image responses are signature-validated so HTML/JSON error pages cannot masquerade as captured
  images.
- Video candidate matching is ownership-safe, reducing the risk of one post's MP4 being attached
  to another post or quote.
- Focused posts can explicitly choose **Save full thread**.
- Reply exports can include the parent post as conversation context.
- A redesigned local library saves organized HTML, LLM Markdown, and media bundles.
- One-off HTML + Markdown downloads now arrive in one clearly labelled ZIP.
- The MV3 popup and in-page menus received clearer status, keyboard navigation, focus handling,
  dark mode, and reduced-motion support.
- Optional AI-readable sharing requires confirmation, has an expiry, excludes raw video, and is
  separate from the private local archive workflow.

Known limits: content deleted or hidden from the logged-in user cannot be reconstructed; HLS-only
video may fall back to a poster and source link; arbitrarily long threads remain best-effort when X
withholds posts. SourceCapsule reports these states instead of silently claiming success.

## Replacement Greasy Fork listing copy

Save X posts, threads, replies, and Articles as portable offline archives and clean AI-readable
Markdown.

### What SourceCapsule preserves

- Main post or Article text, headings, lists, and links
- Images and downloadable MP4 video in the full offline HTML
- Quoted posts rebuilt as selectable cards, including nested quotes, polls, media, and rich links
- Reply context and same-author thread structure
- Capture metadata, source links, byte sizes, hashes, and honest incomplete-content warnings

### Save options

- **Save to library:** organized per-post folders with offline HTML, `.llm.md`, and `media/`
- **Download ZIP (HTML + Markdown):** one portable archive containing both companion files
- **Copy clean Markdown:** text and metadata without embedded base64, CSS, or executable HTML
- **Optional AI-readable link:** uploaded only after confirmation, expires automatically, and does
  not include raw video

### Reliability

Strict Export is on by default. SourceCapsule checks for missing quote links/content and failed
media, retries recovery, and blocks silent incomplete downloads until you choose what to do.

### Privacy

Local saves remain in your browser-selected folder or download ZIP and require no account. Nothing
is uploaded unless you explicitly choose and confirm **Create AI readable link**. Shared links are
unlisted, expire after the selected period, and omit raw video.

### Honest limits

SourceCapsule can only preserve content available to your logged-in X session. Deleted, private,
restricted, HLS-only, or withheld content may be represented by an explicit note, poster, source
link, or incomplete warning rather than fabricated content.

Free and open source: https://github.com/wolfgang-aura/SourceCapsule
